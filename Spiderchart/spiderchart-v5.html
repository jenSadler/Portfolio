Map Your Course Learning Types

Describe the learning types used in your current course design with a scale from 0-10 (0=not used, 10=used a lot).

After completing the storyboarding activity, describe the learning types that you will use in your future course design on a scale of 0-10.

To save your chart, use the file > print menu, and save as a pdf. Note: if you are having printing errors, ensure the window is relatively small, refresh the page, and then try printing again.
